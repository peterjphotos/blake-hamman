Blake Hamman — Actor

A performer of
range and depth

From Shakespearean patriarchs to musical grotesques, from Sondheim to farce, Blake Hamman's acting career spans a decade of professional stage work in San Antonio. Recognized by the Alamo Theatre Arts Council across five consecutive seasons, his work is marked by physical specificity, emotional commitment, and an instinct for the comic and the devastating alike.
