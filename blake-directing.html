The Art of
Direction

From world premieres to award-winning revivals — a career spent illuminating story, character, and the lived human experience on stages across Texas.

Artistic Vision
Theatre is the most immediate art form — it lives and dies in the present tense, in the breath between actor and audience. My work as a director starts with the question: what does this story demand of us, right now, in this room?

The world premiere production of this original work — recognized by the Alamo Theatre Arts Council with its highest directing honor.
